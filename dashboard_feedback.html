What's in the Dashboard

Sales Snapshot Tabs

5 views: Today/WTD/MTD/YTD, Weekly Rollups, Monthly Rollups, Year-over-Year, and 2026 by Month. Each shows 10 KPIs with vs-LY comparisons.

8 Interactive Charts

Daily sales + moving averages, weekly projections, ad spend vs revenue, AUR & velocity, traffic funnel, product mix, and YoY monthly bars.

Profitability Waterfall

Visual breakdown from Gross Revenue through COGS, FBA Fees, Referral, Ad Spend, to Net Profit with percentage-of-revenue labels.

Product Performance

All 36 SKUs with Revenue, Units, AUR, Ad Spend, ACOS/TACOS, Net Profit, Margin, and trend indicators. Toggle Top 25 or All Items.

FBA Inventory Status

Stock levels, inbound shipments, reserved units, days of supply, and restock alerts (Critical / Low / OK / Excess) for every product.

GolfGen Branding

Fully styled with the GolfGen brand guide — teal/navy/orange palette, DM Serif Display + Sora + Space Grotesk typography, gradient accents.
